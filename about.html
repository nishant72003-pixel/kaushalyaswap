About Kaushalya Swap

Kaushalya Swap is a community-driven platform where people can share their skills and learn from others. Our mission is to make learning accessible, collaborative, and rewarding by enabling skill exchange instead of traditional payment.

Our Values

Collaboration

We believe in learning together, building bonds through shared knowledge.

Accessibility

Everyone has something to teach and something to learn, regardless of background.

Growth

Skill-sharing encourages continuous personal and professional growth.

Who We Are

We are a passionate group of learners and educators committed to making knowledge exchange simple, fun, and effective. Together, we aim to create a global learning network.
